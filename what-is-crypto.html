What is
Cryptocurrency?

Think of it as digital money that works like email — fast, borderless, and controlled by math instead of banks.

Progress: 1 of 15

Imagine money that works like email

Before email, sending messages took days. Today, you can send an email instantly to anyone, anywhere, for free.

Cryptocurrency is the same idea — but for money.

Traditional Money vs Cryptocurrency

Traditional Money
  • Controlled by banks and governments
  • Can be frozen or censored
  • Slow international transfers
Cryptocurrency
  • No single controller
  • You control your own money
  • Fast, cheap, borderless transfers

Three Core Ideas

💰

1. Digital Money

Money that exists purely on the internet.

🌐

2. Decentralized

No bank or government is in charge.

🔒

3. Secure by Mathematics

Protected by strong cryptography.

Key Takeaway

Cryptocurrency is digital money that doesn't need a bank. It gives more freedom — but also more personal responsibility.

Quick Summary

  • Digital money
  • Decentralized
  • Secure by math
