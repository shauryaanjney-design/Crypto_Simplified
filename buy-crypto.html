How to Buy Crypto Safely

Step-by-step guide for beginners in India — from choosing an exchange to withdrawing to your own wallet.

Progress: 7 of 15

Step-by-Step: Buying Crypto Safely (India Focus)

1

Choose a Reputable Exchange

Start with regulated Indian platforms like CoinDCX, WazirX, or Mudrex. Look for strong security history and FIU-IND registration.

2

Complete KYC

Upload PAN, Aadhaar (or passport) and complete verification. This is mandatory and helps with tax compliance.

3

Deposit Money

Use UPI or bank transfer. Most Indian exchanges support instant UPI deposits with low or zero fees.

Tip: Start with a small test deposit first.

4

Buy Your First Crypto

Beginners should start with Bitcoin or Ethereum. Double-check the pair (e.g., BTC/INR) before confirming.

5

Withdraw to Your Own Wallet

Never leave large amounts on exchanges. Transfer to your personal wallet (preferably hardware).

⚠️ Always test withdrawal with a tiny amount first.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • ✕ Leaving large amounts on the exchange
  • ✕ Buying during extreme hype without research
  • ✕ Clicking suspicious links
  • ✕ Ignoring transaction fees and taxes

Key Takeaway

Safety is more important than speed. Start small, verify every step, and always move your crypto to a wallet you control.

Quick Summary

  • Choose regulated Indian exchanges
  • Complete full KYC
  • Start with small amounts
  • Always withdraw to your own wallet
