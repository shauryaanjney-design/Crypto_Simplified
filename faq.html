Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How much should a beginner invest in crypto?

A: Only invest money you can afford to lose completely. Many start with ₹1,000–5,000 per month using DCA. Focus on learning before increasing amounts.

Q: Which wallet is best for beginners?

A: Start with a reputable mobile wallet for small amounts. For larger holdings, move to a hardware wallet (Ledger or Trezor). Remember: Not your keys, not your coins.

Q: Is crypto safe?

A: The technology is secure, but your security depends on your habits. Scams and hacks are common. Follow the security rules from Lesson 5 strictly.

Q: Can the government ban crypto in India?

A: The government has not banned it and has introduced regulations and taxation instead. Always stay updated with official announcements.

Q: Should I tell my family about my crypto investments?

A: Only if they are financially literate and supportive. Many prefer to keep it private until they have a clear long-term plan.

Crypto Glossary (A–Z)

Blockchain

A decentralized, transparent, and immutable digital ledger.

Wallet

Software or hardware that stores your private keys and lets you manage crypto.

Private Key / Seed Phrase

The secret code that proves ownership. Never share it.

DYOR

Do Your Own Research — always verify information yourself.

FOMO

Fear Of Missing Out — emotional buying at peak prices.

HODL

Holding crypto long-term instead of selling during volatility.

VDA

Virtual Digital Asset — official Indian government term for crypto.

Smart Contract

Self-executing code that runs automatically on blockchain.
